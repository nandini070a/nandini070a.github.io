About Oil & Gas Knowledge Centre

The Oil & Gas Knowledge Centre is a one stop repository for:

  Industry News - Daily news on Oil & Gas

 Sector Knowledge - Reports and statistics on Oil & Gas in India as well as abroad.

 Companies - Company Profiles, Financials of the companies, Company Reports and CV's of Company Personnel.

 Policies - Various accounting, taxation and regulatory policies applicable for the sector.

 PwC O&G Practice - Client Wins in Oil & Gas , Proposals, Engagements that we have undertaken in India as well as abroad and list of global and Indian CV's of Oil & Gas personnel in PwC.

 Need Information - This feature of the Oil & Gas Knowledge Centre allows any user to log in a request (pertaining to the Oil & Gas Knowledge Centre) on to the Knowledge Centre. This request would be responded in the given timeline by the Industry Knowledge Broker, Vikram Nair.
