PwC’s 2006 Global Energy, Utilities and Mining (EU&M) Conference

Cycling in Stanley Park
Suspension Bridge Capilano
Our Power and Oil and Gas team in India, participated in PwC’s 2006 Global Energy, Utilities and Mining (EU&M) Conference held in Vancouver. The theme of this year’s conference was ‘To 2030 and Beyond - Resources and Technology to Fuel the Future’. Representing the Indian team were Yogesh Daruka (GRID), Sunil Kumar (GRID), Tushar Sud (GRID), Deepak Mahurkar (Oil &Gas), Manish Baghla (Oil & Gas), Ashutosh Chaturvedi (TRS) and Pravi Bansal (FAS).  The team made presentations on ‘Recent Trends and Opportunities’ in India Oil & Gas and Utilities sectors.

PwC’s Global Transfer Pricing Conference 2006

PwC’s annual global transfer pricing conference was held in Shanghai, China, during October 06, where transfer pricing partners across our global network gathered for internal workshops. This was followed by a for our key clients, which provided a platform to meet their peers and interact with our experts. The conference was attended by over 500 people, including around 250 clients. Shyamal Mukherjee, Indraneel Roy Choudhury, Rahul Mitra and Sanjay Tolia, represented India and presented papers on topical issues.
