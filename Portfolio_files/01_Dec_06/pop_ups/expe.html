PwC’s 2006 Global Energy, Utilities and Mining (EU&M) Conference

The Conference included Account Planning Workshops (APW) for key accounts.  Channel 2 accounts were meant for all which included BP, ConocoPhillips, BHP Billiton, Halliburton, Total and Baker Hughes.  The Channel 1 account workshops were restricted to engagement teams.  A roster of outstanding speakers from other consulting companies, clients and various practices of PwC made thought provoking presentations pertinent to the energy practice.  Special breakfast sessions and traditional networking dinners were also hosted.

It was a good networking and learning opportunity and the icing on the cake was the chilly morning runs, the cycling in the Stanley Park and the cheerful time we had amongst ourselves. “It helped understanding our perspectives in line with our global strengths. These giant British Colombia's Totem Poles (structures) akin our global networks, urges us to further harness our energies to grow faster, better and bigger than competition. As we developed rapport within the global network, this would go a long way in providing valued services to our priority Indian clients going global”, feels Team India.
